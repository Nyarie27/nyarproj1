#Does the director influence the popularity of the movie
def groupGenres(column, dataFrame):
    genres= dataFrame[column].str.cat(sep = '|')
    genres = pd.Series(genres.split('|'))
    groupedByCount = genres.value_counts(ascending = False)
    return groupedByCount

Conclusions¶

Does the budget determine the profit?¶

It shows that in most cases budget determines the profit since there is positive fair relationship as shown on the scatterplot.

Which attributes had the most strong relationship with revenue?¶

Popularity and budget had the strongest positive relationship with revenue.

Which are the most popular movie genres?¶

Drama is the most popular genre followed by comedy.

Which period produced many movies?¶

From 2008 more movies were produced
